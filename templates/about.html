Hello !!!

Our mission at AMHS is to revolutionize the landscape of mental healthcare by addressing the intellectual and emotional depth of mental health issues while acknowledging the significance of ethnic and cultural practices that can lead to culture-specific problems. We are committed to achieving the following key objectives:

  • Ethical Excellence
  • In-Depth Understanding
  • Cultural Relevance
  • Education and Awareness
  • Accessibility and Inclusivity

Megha Jain, a Clinical Psychologist, specializes in a wide range of therapeutic techniques and has extensive experience in treating various mental disorders. Aryavardhan Jain, the Technical Director at AMHS, is a computer science student and web developer focused on creating accessible online mental healthcare platforms. Together, they bring a blend of technological innovation and clinical expertise to AMHS, aiming to address the complex challenges of modern mental health care.
